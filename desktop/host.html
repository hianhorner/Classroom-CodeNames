Ready the room with one click.

This desktop app starts the built-in classroom server, saves data on this computer, opens the classroom in your browser, and gives you the LAN link students can use on phones, tablets, and other laptops.

  • The first launch may trigger a macOS or Windows firewall prompt for local network access.
