How to Play

  • 1

    Two teams compete to identify their hidden words.

  • 2

    One player gives a one-word clue that connects multiple words on the board.

  • 3

    Teammates try to guess the correct words based on the clue.

  • 4

    Avoid selecting the assassin tile.

  • 5

    The first team to identify all their words wins.
